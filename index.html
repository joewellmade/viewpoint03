Viewpoint specialise in the hospitality industry and deliver high quality financial information to our customers. We work quickly and concisely, side-by-side with some of London’s most exciting hospitality businesses, providing owners and management teams with in depth financial insight that is integral to their growth and continued success.

What We Do:

  • Management Reporting
  • Bookkeeping
  • Financial Projections Modelling
  • Cash Flow Forecasting
  • Consulting
  • System Implementation
  • Training

Our Team

David Grant

Client Lead

As founder and managing director, David has, and continues to be the catalyst by which Viewpoint thrives. Central to his guiding principle is the belief that solid long term relationships are the foundation of any successful enterprise. Unwavering enthusiasm and conviviality combine to help build a client base of London’s most exciting new bars and restaurants. David’s professionalism and experience ensure they remain.
